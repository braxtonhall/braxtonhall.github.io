# no one's interrogated you yet

> our host Dave  
> warmed up to us over coffee  
> asking questions  
> about the significant ones
>
> first Megha
>
> then Jackie
>
> then me  
> too quiet in the corner
>
> "no one's interrogated you yet"
>
> I gave him a non-answer
>
> I could never admit to humanity

text written 2020 august 14
